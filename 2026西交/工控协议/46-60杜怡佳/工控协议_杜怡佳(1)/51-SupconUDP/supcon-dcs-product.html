Overview

What is OMC

OMC (Intelligent Operation Management & Control System) is SUPCON’s advanced industrial control and management platform that unifies process control, optimization, and operations within a single system. It combines Industrial Operating System, Industrial AIoT, advanced networking, intelligent optimization, and model prediction technologies to achieve autonomous and efficient plant operation. Designed with a flexible, reliable, and secure architecture, OMC enables integrated joint control of all production equipment, driving the transformation from automation to autonomy in process industries.

High Availability (HA)

The system is engineered with no single point of failure, ensuring continuous operation even if individual components fail.

Openness and Interoperability

Supports various standard industrial communication protocols, including PROFIBUS, PROFINET, Modbus, FF, HART, and EtherNet/IP, allowing seamless integration with heterogeneous systems.
